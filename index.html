The End?

Well, it's been a long time coming but we've finally shut things down. The servers and forums are empty so there's no need to keep them up.

We've had over a decade of fun. Realisms with Arch, 1 vs 1 scoutzknivez tournaments, sig tournaments, Ventrilo hangouts, VSH nights... It's been an interesting ride seeing the different generations and games come through, watching our userbase expand and change.

Many thanks to all the founders, admins, VC members, clan members, and homies that made this community great. Be sure to join our steam group if you haven't already in case you want to stay in touch.
